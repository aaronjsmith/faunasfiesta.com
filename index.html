Save money · Fight waste · Eat well

Great deals on food that deserves a second chance

We're an independent store on a simple mission: help you pay less for groceries while keeping good food out of the trash. We source scratch-and-dent items, surplus stock, and products pulled from shelves because of sell-by or best-by dates—not because they're spoiled. When the label is wrong for the landfill but right for your pantry, you'll find it here.

Food is food—prices shouldn't be fiction

Big stores often toss or offload perfectly usable groceries because of a dented box, a seasonal label change, or a date that scares people—even when the product is still fine. We buy that inventory, price it fairly, and pass the savings on to you. Less waste, fuller carts, same dinner.

What you'll find

Inventory changes fast—that's how rescue retail works. Expect a treasure-hunt aisle: what's here today might be gone tomorrow, and something new always rotates in.

Scratch & dent

Dinged boxes, bumped labels, and "ugly" packaging—the stuff stores can't shelve at full price, even when the food inside is untouched.

Short & misunderstood dates

Items near or past sell-by / best-by dates when they're still safe and delicious—we'll help you understand what the date actually means.

Surplus & overstocks

Extra cases, discontinued lines, and closeouts we buy for less so you pay less.

Pantry & everyday eats

Canned goods, dry goods, snacks, drinks, and staples—perfect for stocking up when the price is right.
