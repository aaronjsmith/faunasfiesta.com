About Fauna's Fiesta

Famous food… fabulous prices… because food should be fun!

We've searched to find the highest-quality food from restaurant suppliers—with a twist: we buy it from their overstock, so we can offer incredibly low pricing. That means a savings of 50% or more for you, without coupons. What would you eat differently if food was this cheap and higher quality than the grocery store?

Many years ago, my husband used to joke with our customers and tell them that we had so many kids that we bought food by the pallet… well, actually, that wasn't just a joke. When you can find this kind of food for these prices, you (we) buy it by the pallet, stock what you can in your kitchen, and preserve what you can for your larder. We've always had a food storage system that we are continually using. Because of this, we are personally familiar with effective methods to preserve and use many foods. What we don't personally know, we have learned where to find it out.

We hope that you will take advantage of our low prices to stock up in your kitchen—and also for a rainy day—because rainy days always come. Now you can look forward to them!

  • 50%+ savings — restaurant-supplier quality, overstock pricing
  • No coupons needed — the savings are baked into how we buy
  • Stock smart — we're glad to share what we've learned about preserving and using what you buy

Pantry & everyday eats

Canned goods, dry goods, snacks, drinks, and staples—perfect for stocking up when the price is right.
